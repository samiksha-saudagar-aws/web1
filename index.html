Beautiful Flowers

Flowers make the world more beautiful and come in many types and colors. Here are some common types of flowers:

1. Rose

Roses are known for their beauty and fragrance. They come in various colors like red, pink, and white.

2. Sunflower

Sunflowers are bright yellow and always face the sun. They symbolize happiness and positivity.

3. Tulip

Tulips bloom in spring and come in many vibrant colors. They are simple yet elegant flowers.
